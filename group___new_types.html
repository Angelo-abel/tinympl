template<std::intmax_t Num, std::intmax_t Den>
using tinympl::rational = typedef typename detail::make_rational<Num,Den>::type

Convenience wrapper around std::ratio to automatically reduce num and den to coprime factors.

std::is_same< std::ratio<4,2>, std::ratio<2,1> >::value is false, while std::is_same< rational<4,2>, rational<2,1> >::value is true.

rational forwards to std::ratio. The comparison functionals plus, minus, multiplies, divides, negate, equal_to and less are specialized to work transparently on std::ratio.
